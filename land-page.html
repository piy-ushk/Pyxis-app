User: What's the derivative of x²sin(x)?
Pixio: Let's solve this using the product rule:
Pixio: 1. First, let's identify our functions: u = x², v = sin(x).
2. The product rule states: d/dx(uv) = u(dv/dx) + v(du/dx).
3. For u = x², du/dx = 2x
4. For v = sin(x), dv/dx = cos(x)
5. Applying the product rule: d/dx(x²sin(x)) = x²cos(x) + sin(x)(2x)
6. Simplifying: d/dx(x²sin(x)) = x²cos(x) + 2xsin(x)
